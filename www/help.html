Race Design

Primary Racial Traits

Melconians

pros: built in scanner on every ship
Your people know how to do a lot of things and do them well but as a whole they don't speshalize in anything.

Kender

pros: built in cloak on every ship, stronger cloaking units, componets that can steal cargo
Your people are stelthy, and good at being stelthy; they will pick up just about whatever they find wether it's scrap medal or minerals from inside a ship's caro bay.

Gaerhule

pros: bigger hiper denial than all othe rases, faster minelayers than all other rases, dual armor/shield, better planetary shields
cons: no bombs that avoid defences
You are great at defending your territory, at keeping the enemy away and destoying him if he gets too close. Your people aren't agressive but firce when fighting off invaders.

TANSTAAFL

pros: orbital component that can fling astoroids across the galexy at astounding speeds
Your starbases can fling astoroids big or small at speeds up to hiper 17. You can use thies astoroids to bombard enemy planets or to trasport minerals from one world to another.

Pa'anuri

pros: biggest star base hull
You live in starbases which means that if the starbase is destoyed your people die in the vacuum of space. It also means that your max population is determand by your starbase not the habitablity of the world. Because you live in a starbase you cannot build factories, mines, or power plants. Instead of building factories you get production capasity based on your reparir ablity. Instead of building mines to get minerals you do bario genisis to create them. Instead of building power plants to get energy you get it from your populaton and an orbital componet.

Aku'Ultani

pros: cheaper / more powerful wepons, bigger ship hulls
cons: no mine layers or hipper denial
You are great at going out attacking the enemy before they can get to you. Your people are aggressive wanting to go out and attack to the point of not careing about defences.

Formics

pros: small fast colony ship, doble groth rate
cons: no star gates, no mat tras, half the max popualtion
Your people grow like crazy and spread across the galexy like crazy; and then, when they are all over the place, overwhelm every one else with their supirior numbers.

Patryns

pros: higher capasity stargates, can gate colonists
You are great at moving across the galexy through your stargates even to other's stargates. You can gate anything if it is small eough. You can gate your colonizers, trasports, ships of war, utility ships, and everything else.

Halleyforms

pros: all planets that are habitable are 100%
Your people are happy to live almost anywhere, the only things holding them back are phisical space and extreem condisions that will kill them.
